## üß© 5. Logo Concepts

### Logo Style Options

**1. Minimal Icon + Wordmark**
- A square or rounded rectangle representing a "desk"
- With a small circle or dot inside representing "core"
- Example: a cube with a glowing center

**2. Abstract C + D**
- Interlocked "C" and "D" forming a workspace icon
- Looks clean and brandable

**3. Geometric Core**
- A central dot surrounded by lines
- Symbolizing "core logic" or "control center"
